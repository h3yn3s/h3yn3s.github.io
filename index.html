You are the boss of a small company, but may rise to a great business-idol. You gain cash by clicking on your cash. After a while you can hire your first workers. You need to pay them and they will earn cash for you. After 10 new workers hired you can grab yourself an energy drink to double the cash you make by clicking.

Further information can be found in the Stats.

Happy clicking.
